Lyfe By The Horns Page

"Life By The Horns" is an immersive digital platform designed to host high-impact podcast and video content centered around business, family, health, and mindset. The primary objective of this UI/UX project was to visually capture the direct, unfiltered attitude of the show, guided by the host's core philosophy that motivation is cheap and execution is everything. To achieve this, I implemented a dark mode aesthetic with high-contrast typography and vibrant color accents, reflecting a strong, distraction-free environment that aligns perfectly with their "no fluff, just pure execution" messaging. The site's architecture prioritizes immediate action by featuring a main episode player at the top to maximize initial user retention. Furthermore, the navigation was strategically structured into clear content pillars alongside a dedicated "Meet The Host" section, which was designed to build visual trust and highlight the founders' extensive background in sales, marketing, and fitness.
